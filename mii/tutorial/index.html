※⁜ welcome! this is a tutorial on how to make a .charinfo mii special/have gold pants! ⁜※

⁜ first, you need: ⁜

• a modded Nintendo Switch (or a Nintendo Switch emulator, recommend using Ryujinx!)

• a hex editor

• have the MiiPort homebrew app installed for your modded Nintendo Switch or Nintendo Switch emulator.

• you must have a .charinfo mii, here's a charinfo mii that you can use: Mii

※⁜ now that you have all of this stuff, let's get into making a special .charinfo mii!⁜※

⁜ first, go to your hex editor, open your charinfo mii that you just downloaded inside of your hex editor. ⁜

※ you may see this screen: ※

and wonder... where's the gold pants state for the charinfo mii?

well, we will figure that out right now!

you need to find text called "0B" in the hex editor, and when you found it, go to the third instance of "0B", and when you found it, you may see this number: "00"

we need to edit that number to "01", but why?
well, charinfo mii's have these normal/special pants (aka gold pants)

00 means... normal pants!
01 means... special pants!

※ now that you have edited the number "00" to "01", you need to save the hex edited charinfo, and when you saved it, put your edited charinfo mii in your modded Nintendo Switch's SD card or from your modded Nintendo Switch emulator's virtual SD card to this path: ※

sdmc:/MiiPort/miis

⁜ now, open the MiiPort homebrew app from your modded Nintendo Switch or from your Nintendo Switch emulator, and select the example.charinfo mii. ⁜

a message pop up on the top right will say something, if it says something about "The Mii has successfully imported!",
it means the charinfo mii you just edited successfully got added in your mii maker!
if not, you may have done something wrong...

※⁜ go to the mii maker from your Nintendo Switch's settings, and click the "Special" mii you just imported, and... ⁜※

※⁜ congrats! you just added a special mii to your Nintendo Switch! 😱 ⁜※
